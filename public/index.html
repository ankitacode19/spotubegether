⚠️ How this works safely Your sp_dc cookie is sent to the SyncSpace server only to get a short-lived Spotify access token — exactly the same thing your browser does when you open Spotify. The cookie is never stored or logged. Only the token is kept in memory for your session.

🔑 Connect your Spotify

1. Open open.spotify.com and log in.
2. Open DevTools (F12) → Application → Cookies → open.spotify.com
3. Find the cookie named sp_dc and copy its value.

Your cookie is used only to get a token. It is never stored or logged.
